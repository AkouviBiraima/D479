Getting Here & Around

Everything you need to know about traveling to and around Taniti

✈️

By Air

Taniti is served by a small airport accommodating small jets and propeller planes. The airport is being expanded to accommodate larger jets in the coming years.

🚢

By Cruise Ship

A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Check the cruise schedule for upcoming arrivals and plan your island visit accordingly.

🏥 Safety & Health

Medical Facilities

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics located in Taniti City. The hospital has many multilingual employees. For emergencies, head directly to the hospital.

Safety Tips

Violent crime is very rare. Be mindful of pickpocketing in tourist areas. Keep valuables secure and use hotel safes when available. Emergency services are available 24/7.
